About The Meridian Grand

The Meridian Grand stands as a testament to luxury and sophistication, offering an unparalleled hospitality experience in the heart of the city. Since our opening in 1985, we have been committed to providing guests with exceptional service, elegant accommodations, and memorable experiences.

Our hotel features 200 beautifully appointed rooms and suites, each designed with meticulous attention to detail. From our marble-clad lobbies to our world-class spa and dining facilities, every aspect of The Meridian Grand reflects our commitment to excellence.

We pride ourselves on our personalized service, ensuring that every guest feels valued and pampered throughout their stay. Our dedicated team of hospitality professionals is available 24/7 to cater to your every need.
